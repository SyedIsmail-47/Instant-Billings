Who We Are


Physicians Revenue Group, Inc provides end-to-end Revenue Cycle Management services that enable healthcare organizations to attain better practice outcomes. In addition, we offer services to independent practices, specialty practices, single and multispecialty group practices, hospitals, nursing homes, and emergency centers. As a result, it leads to operational efficiency and financial independence.

Our revenue cycle management services ensure that practices achieve accelerated practice growth. Also, it brings financial stability, and improved patient experience to progress in the value-based era. Further, practices need to act carefully to attain greater scores under the latest MIPS/MACRA rules. In fact, we enable you to add value to a patient’s life. Consequently, you gain incentives in the value-based healthcare model.

In addition, based in Downers Grove, IL with offices and clients across the country, we are built upon the vision to transform the healthcare system. Thus, we offer comprehensive solutions to practices. Therefore, our focus has been on improving practice outcomes through operational efficiency. As a result, it allows healthcare providers to serve their communities with higher quality patient care. So, that’s how we help you!

Moreover, working together with our industry partners, we are equipped to meet the breadth of healthcare landscape requirements. In addition, our staff is comprised of more than 5,000 business strategists, Revenue Cycle Consultants, clinical coordinators, compliance officers, IT and infrastructure developers. So, the depth of expertise in the relevant industry enables PRG to deliver all services under one roof.
